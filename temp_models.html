Models

Choosing a model

If you're not sure where to start, use gpt-5.5, our flagship model for complex reasoning and coding. If you're optimizing for latency and cost, choose a smaller variant like gpt-5.4-mini or gpt-5.4-nano.

All latest OpenAI models support text and image input, text output, multilingual capabilities, and vision. Models are available via the Responses API and our Client SDKs.

Frontier models

Start with gpt-5.5 for complex reasoning and coding, or choose gpt-5.4-mini and gpt-5.4-nano for lower-latency, lower-cost workloads.
